History

Adopt-A-Saurus was founded in the heart of Dino Valley, born from a shared love of prehistoric creatures and a deep concern for their wellbeing. What started as a small rescue shelter for abandoned baby dinosaurs quickly grew into a full sanctuary dedicated to rehabilitation, care, and finding loving forever homes for every dino in need. Over the years, we have welcomed hundreds of dinosaurs — from gentle giants to feisty raptors — and placed them with caring families across the region. Our story is one of passion, community, and countless tiny roars that found their forever homes.

🐾

VISION

To be the leading dinosaur adoption sanctuary in the region, creating a world where every prehistoric creature is valued, cared for, and given the chance to thrive in a loving home.

🐾

MISSION

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dinosaurs of all species through compassionate care, community education, and responsible adoption practices — ensuring every dino finds the family they deserve.

🐾

OBJECTIVES

  • Rescue abandoned, injured, or neglected dinosaurs and provide immediate medical attention.
  • Maintain a safe and enriching environment for all dinosaurs in our care.
  • Screen and match prospective adopters to ensure the best fit for both dino and family.
  • Educate the public on responsible dinosaur ownership and prehistoric creature welfare.
  • Partner with local communities and schools to promote dino awareness and conservation.
  • Continuously improve our facilities and care practices through research and expert collaboration.
